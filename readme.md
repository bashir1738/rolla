Rolla


Rotating Savings, Reimagined Onchain



Rolla is a mobile savings platform that digitalizes the traditional African rotating savings system — ajo, esusu, adashe — and combines it with individual yield-bearing vaults. No organizer. No trust required. The rules are in code.


The problem

Rotating savings groups have existed across Africa for generations. They break for the same reasons every time — the organizer disappears with the pot, a member stops paying with no consequence, there is no record of who paid what, and the money earns nothing while it waits. Rolla fixes all of this.


How it works

Circles
Create or join a rotating savings group. Every member contributes a fixed amount on a set schedule. When all contributions land, the smart contract automatically releases the full pot to the next member in the queue. No admin, no manual release, no single person holding the funds.

Vaults
Save solo between payout cycles. Deposit any EVM token — it gets converted to USDT and deployed to Aave V3 for yield. Choose from three tiers based on how long you want to lock:


Flex — 4.5% APR, no lock, withdraw anytime
Growth — 9.2% APR, 90-day lock
Power — 14.8% APR, 365-day lock


When your vault matures, claim in USDT or any EVM-compatible token directly to your wallet.

History
A full onchain record of every contribution, payout, deposit, and interest earned — tied to your wallet address and verifiable by anyone.


Who it is for

Urban professionals in Nigeria and across West Africa aged 22–40 who already participate in ajo but want a version that is trustless, transparent, and puts idle savings to work.
